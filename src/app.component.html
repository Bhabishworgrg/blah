About Me

From a young age, I was captivated by the stories clothes could tell. My journey into fashion design is a pursuit of that narrative magic, blending timeless elegance with a modern, rebellious spirit. Each piece I create is more than just a garment; it's a piece of a larger story, designed to empower and inspire.

I specialize in sustainable couture, using ethically sourced materials to craft bespoke pieces that are both beautiful and conscious. My work has been featured in several independent fashion shows, and I am always seeking new collaborations and challenges.
